Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when you create an account, along with the expenses, categories, amounts, and dates you log while using Spendly.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide the core features of Spendly: storing your expenses, generating spending summaries, and letting you sign in to your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in our database and protected with reasonable technical safeguards. Passwords are never stored in plain text. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell your personal or financial data to third parties. We may rely on trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting) to operate the service, and those providers only process data as needed to keep Spendly running.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach out to us through the contact details provided on your account page.
